Giraffe

Giraffes tongues are 21 inches long, that's almost 2 feet!!

  • Scientific Name: Giraffa camelopardalis
  • Average Height: 14 - 20 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Habitat: Grassy plains and open woodlands in tropical and subtropical regions

Giraffes are the world's tallest mammals. The giraffe's distinguishing characteristics are its extremely long neck and legs, its horn-like ossicones, and its distinctive coat patterns.
